## FAQ

### What counts as a Fleet Mission?
A Fleet Mission is a single coordinated task where multiple AI agents work together. Each mission launch counts as one, regardless of how many agents participate.

### What counts as a Roundtable Session?
A Roundtable Session is a single AI discussion. Sessions can have unlimited messages and participants.

### Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! Upgrade instantly, downgrade at the end of your billing cycle.

### Do unused credits roll over?
Fleet Missions and Roundtable Sessions reset monthly. AI credits purchased as add-ons never expire.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes! Start a 14-day free trial of Pro with no credit card required.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfer for Enterprise.
